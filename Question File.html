  2. What is the unit of frequency? A. Ohm B. Hertz C. Ampere D. Volt

  3. What is the main function of a fuse in an electronic circuit? A. To store energy B. To amplify signals C. To protect the circuit from overcurrent D. To convert AC to DC

  4. What is the symbol for a resistor in a circuit diagram? A. A straight line B. A zigzag line C. A circle with a cross inside D. Two parallel lines

  5. What is the basic function of an operational amplifier in an electronic circuit? A. To resist current flow B. To store energy C. To amplify or switch electronic signals D. To convert AC to DC

  6. What is the unit of power? A. Ohm B. Farad C. Watt D. Volt

  7. What is the main function of a switch in an electronic circuit? A. To control the flow of current B. To store energy C. To amplify signals D. To convert AC to DC

  8. What is the symbol for a capacitor in a circuit diagram? A. A straight line B. A triangle with a line at one end C. Two parallel lines D. A circle with a cross inside

  9. What is the unit of electric charge? A. Ohm B. Farad C. Ampere D. Coulomb

  10. What is the main function of a battery in an electronic circuit? A. To resist current flow B. To provide electrical energy C. To amplify signals D. To convert AC to DC

Answer Key:

  1. A. To convert AC to DC
  2. B. Hertz
  3. C. To protect the circuit from overcurrent
  4. B. A zigzag line
  5. C. To amplify or switch electronic signals
  6. C. Watt
  7. A. To control the flow of current
  8. C. Two parallel lines
  9. D. Coulomb
  10. B. To provide electrical energy
